About Me


Scientist, Artist, and Writer
Mir Ishruna Muniyat is a science researcher, artist, and writer. She was born in Dhaka, Bangladesh, where she obtained a Bachelor degree in Pharmacy and a Masters degree in Clinical Pharmacy and Pharmacology. Ishruna moved to Calgary, Alberta to pursue a Master of Science degree with specialization in Biophysical Chemistry.
